Professional Organizations

ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery chapter at UCLA is the largest computer science organization at UCLA. They have 8 smaller clubs that that make up the organization ranging from AI to cybersecurity to gaming.

Upsilon Pi Epsilon

Upsilon Pi Epsilon is the computer science honors society at UCLA. To get in, students must have a minimum GPA of around 3.7 to be nominated to join the society. They host tutoring sessions throughout each quarter and also have company information sessions.

DEV X

DEV X is a project based organization at UCLA. They focus on projects to be used by their fellow Bruins here on campus. They have done projects such as a dating app and an app to find people to eat with in the dining halls.

LUG @ UCLA

The Linux Users Group does events for doing projects on Linux servers. They help anyone who is interested install Linux on their computers.


Career Options

Database Administrator

Database Administrators make sure that databases are optimized and secure. They supervise when the databases are modified as well.

Web Developer

Web developers work on making websites. They mainly work with languages like HTML, CSS, and Javascript. They can focus on the content of the pages or more on the style and the looks.

Software Developer

Software developers work on teams to create software applications for consumers to use. This could be applications such as photoshop, Netflix, games and more.

Computer and Information Systems Manager

A computer and information systems manager coordinates computer related activities for a company. A computer and information systems manager could become a CIO, CTO, or an IT director.

Corporations

Google

Google is a multi-faceted company that primarily is used as a search engine. If hired, someone could work on a variety of teams and do front-end or back-end work, hardware work, or work on newer projects like VR. Google's values are opportunity for everyone to learn, succeed, and be heard. Google's mission is to organize the information in the world and make it accessible and useful for everyone. For software engineers at Google they look for people with a B.S. degree in computer science or a similar field, experience in a programming language such as Java, C/C++, or Python, and the ability to communicate with others verbally and in writing.

Facebook

Facebook is a social media company that also owns Instagram, WhatsApp, and Oculus VR. Employees at Facebook might work on multiple projects while they work there, such as spam prevention, user interface, VR and AR, and database work. Facebook's values include equal access to the internet and opportunities for everyone and the ability to experience other places in the world through others. Facebook looks for people with at least a B.S. in Computer Science or a related field, experience with programming in a language such as C/C++, Java, or Python, and other skills depending on the position.

Zynga

Zynga is an online game company that has made games such as FarmVille, Zynga Poker, and Words with Friends. A game team employee at Zynga might work with the Unity engine and do front-end, back-end, or full-stack work. Zynga's values include making games people enjoy playing and each employee being a CEO of their work. Zynga's mission is to connect the world through games. Zynga looks for potential hires to have experience in Unity or other game development platforms like Unreal, understanding of CPU and GPU optimization, and good problem solving skills.

Lockheed Martin

Lockheed Martin is a defense, security, and advanced systems company that does work with the United States military and NASA. A programmer at Lockheed might work on creating systems for missiles, AI, or web development. Lockheed's values are doing what's right, respect, and performing with excellence. Lockheed's mission is advance scientific discovery and come up with solutions to complex problems. Lockheed looks people with skills in C++, real-time programming, Python, web development, and embedded software development.
